RECEIPT AND ROUTING OF COMMUNICATION/DOCUMENT

1

The client shall submit their transmittal or request letter along with the required documents attached.

2

The personnel at the receiving desk shall review the content of the letter.

3

Upon completion of the review, the personnel shall affix a received stamp to the document, followed by signing and dating it.

4

The document shall be recorded in the logbook and computer. Afterwards, a routing slip is to be attached and forwarded to the city engineer.

5

Prior to returning it to the receiving desk personnel, the city engineer will thoroughly review and assign the document with instructions.

6

The receiving personnel will update the information in the logbook and computer.

7

If necessary, a photocopy of the document will be produced.

8

Routing of the documents to its concerned division(s) shall commence.

End of Process.
