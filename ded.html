PREPARATION OF DETAILED ENGINEERING DOCUMENTS (DED)

This includes:
1. Preparation of Plan/ Project Design
2. Preparation of Program of Works
3. Project Schedule

1

The client shall secure their request letter and purchase request.

2

Once the letter has been recorded, received, and passed on to the city engineer, it will be routed to the concerned division.

3

When received by the concerned division, the assigned engineer shall conduct site inspection, coordination, measurement, and data gathering.

4

The assigned engineer will then prepare the DED.

5

An elaborate and detailed review of the DED will now commence.

6

Once it is reviewed, the DED will now be recommended for approval.

7

A second review for the document will be done to ensure validity and reliability of the document.

8

After ensuring the reliability and validity of the document, it will be approved and submitted to the records section for retrieval.

End of Process
