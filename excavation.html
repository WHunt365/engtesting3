EXCAVATION PERMIT
An excavation permit is a legal document that
grants permission to dig or excavate in a
specific area, ensuring it's done
safely and properly.
STEP 1

Secure the following requirements:
1. Request letter for excavation
2. Certification from the barangay
3. Certification from Baguio Water District
4. Certification from City Environment & Park Management Office

STEP 2

Assessment of the proposed excavation operation by the City Engineering Office (CEO)

STEP 3

Issuance of the assessment by the City Engineering Office (CEO)

STEP 4

The client shall pay all fees (Excavation Fee & Restoration Fee)

Note:

The Restoration Fee is refundable if the said restoration is done well.

STEP 5

Issuance of the excavation permit by the City Engineering Office (CEO)

STEP 6

Commencement of excavation by the client & inspection by the City Engineering Office (CEO) prior to restoration

STEP 7

Submit excavation permit with documentations before, during, & after operations (2 copies each)

STEP 8

Payment of certification fee by the client

STEP 9

Issuance of certification & voucher by the City Engineering Office (CEO)

End of Process
