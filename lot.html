Public Lot Clearance Process
Clearance for Public Lot Application
Townsite Sales Application (TSA)
Residential Free Patent Application (RFPA)
1

Submit Documents

Submit all necessary documents to the DENR to secure your lot application letter.

2

Survey Division

Once you have the letter from the DENR, go to the Survey Division to schedule your area inspection.

3

Receiving Desk

After you get an appointment, proceed to the receiving desk and hand in lot application letter and sketch plan.

4

Processing

The letter will be processed and sent to the responsible department.

5

City Engineering Office

Return to the City Engineering Office on a scheduled date to accompany the Survey Team for area inspection.

6

Investigation

The assigned engineer will conduct an investigation and verification process specific to the referenced lot.

7

Report & Recommendation

Upon completion, the report and recommendation/clearance will be forwarded to the administrative division.

8

Final Review

The administrative division will review and forward the report to the DENR.

End of Process
