Repair and Maintenance of Roads, Sidewalks, Drainages, etc.

Step 1: The client will submit their letter of request at the receiving desk of the office.

Step 2: The request will be received, recorded, and routed to the Maintenance Division.

Step 3: Once the Maintenance Division receives the letter, they will schedule an assessment of the area and coordinate with the concerned barangay.

Step 4: After the assessment, they will prepare a report and estimate the needed materials for intervention.

Step 5: The Maintenance Division will schedule the repair or maintenance, brief the assigned personnel, and acquire the needed materials.

Step 6: The Maintenance Division will conduct the actual repair or maintenance of the area.

End of Process
