Streetlight, Parklight, and Traffic Light Services

Step 1. Send in a request or submit a letter at the receiving desk. Indicate fixture number or location.

Step 2. Office personnel will record, submit, and forward the request.

Step 3. The Electrical Division shall schedule, prepare, and issue a job order relative to the request.

Step 4. The Electrical Division requests and retrieves materials.

Step 5. Repair, replacement, and troubleshooting shall commence.

End of Process
